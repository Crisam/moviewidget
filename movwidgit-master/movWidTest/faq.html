Products

Q. What is the quality of the items you sell? A. Item quality depends whether movies are in theatres or not. *Rule of thumb: if the Movie is still in theatres or recently out of theatres, quality will be Good cam with or without Chinese subtitles. Otherwise all products will be DVD quality or better


Q. How fast do you get new releases? How often do you update your website
A.We get new releases as soon as a good quality copy is made available and quality assurance tests have been completed by our team. Our website is constantly being updated so please check daily for new releases and important updates


Q. Do you offer albums, software, or Blu-ray movies?
A. No. We only offer products in DVD format.


Q.How can I request products not currently listed on your site?
A. All Questions, Comments, Special Order Requests, or specific Item Requests can be made via email through our “Contact Us” page. Please note that Item Requests are only for movies or series that are currently out, however not listed on the site.


Q. Do your items come with subtitles?
A.None of our items come with subtitles, with the exception of our Japanese or Chinese martial arts movies that come with English subtitles standard. New releases may have Chinese subtitles hardcoded into the movie.


Q. Are your products compatible with all DVD/Blu-Ray devices?
A.All products are compatible with mostly all computer DVD rom drives, and standalone DVD and Blu-Ray players. Please note: Blu-Ray player users have experienced playback problems due to Cinavia copyright protection with some items. Items may not be playable on standard Xbox & PS3 gaming systems.


Payment/Ordering:

Q. When do I pay for my order?

Ans. Payment is due on successful delivery of your order


Q. Do you offer consignment, payment plans, or partial payments?

Ans. Absolutely not.


Q. Do you take Bitcoin, PayPal, or Credit Card payments?

Ans. We are currently only accepting cash as payment at this time. Please note that the exact amount is due at the time of delivery as our drivers do not carry change.


Q. What are the maximum/minimum order sizes?

Ans. We only accept orders of 100 items at a time. Orders less than this will not be accepted. If you would like to order higher quantities per order, please contact us with your special request.


Q. Why can’t I order the exact amount of items that I would like?

Ans. In order to ensure prompt delivery of orders and an efficient manufacturing process, items can only be ordered in increments of (5) only.


Q. Can I Revise/Cancel an order that I submitted?

Ans. Payment is due on successful delivery of your order


Q. When do I pay for my order?

Ans. Once orders are submitted they are processed immediately and cannot be cancelled or revised. Please be sure of your order before submitting it.


Delivery:

Q. How long does it take to deliver my order?

Ans. Customers should expect delivery in 2-3 business days.

Q.How will I know when to expect my delivery?

Ans. Before delivery of your order you will be contacted by our driver to confirm a date, time, and location, that you will be available and able to accept the delivery.

Q. What happens if I am unavailable for my prescheduled delivery?

>Ans. If you are unavailable for a prescheduled delivery the order can be delivered to another individual on your behalf; please let us know via email before the scheduled delivery. Please note: If no one is available at the time of a prescheduled delivery, you will be contacted to reschedule a new delivery date and subsequently charged a $20 redelivery fee (non negotiable).

Returns:

Q. Do you offer returns?

Ans. Only defective items will be eligible for our returns program. Only items wherein the customer complains of disc-skipping are eligible. Items must be returned in original cases with covers and discs to be eligible. We will replace any eligible defective items free of charge (Up to 10 Items per order) to be delivered with your next order delivery.

Q. How do I send you the defective items for return?

Ans. Please give any eligible defective items, along with a note with your contact name, number, and address to our driver upon delivery of an order. Once received we will begin processing your return.
